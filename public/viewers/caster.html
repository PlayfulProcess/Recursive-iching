The Caster

A cast is a path: six lines build the primary hexagram, and its moving lines carry it into another. Read to know yourself, not to be told your fate — relate to the hexagram, never obey it.

Three coins per line: 6 old yin ⅛ · 7 young yang ⅜ · 8 young yin ⅜ · 9 old yang ⅛.

Casting uses your browser's randomness; the yarrow option reproduces the traditional method's unequal probabilities (old yang 3/16, old yin 1/16 — the stalks are biased toward change in yang). Read whole hexagrams across the centuries in the Books. Texts: this repo's public-domain book-grammars.
